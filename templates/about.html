Our Company

Welcome to Otter Labs, your #1 source for all things Music. We're dedicated to giving you the very best musical experience. Otter Labs has come a long way from its beginnings in Oakland. When Alex Harlan first started out, his passion for music drove him to quit his job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the Bay Area, and are thrilled to be a part of the music industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
